Welcome, Everyone! My name is Syahid Izzulhaq, and I am currently pursuing my Master's degree in Economics at the Research School of Economics, the Australian National University (ANU). My research interests lie in macroeconomics, labour economics, agricultural economics, and public economics. I am particularly interested in combining micro-founded general equilibrium models with policy-relevant economic questions. Before joining ANU, I worked as a Junior Advisor at the Executive Office of the President of Indonesia (KSP), where I contributed to policy analysis in issues related agriculture and labour markets.
