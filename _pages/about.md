Welcome, Everyone! My name is Syahid Izzulhaq, and I am currently pursuing my Master's degree in Economics at the Research School of Economics, the Australian National University (ANU). My research interests lie in *macroeconomics, labor economics, agricultural economics, and public economics.* I am particularly interested in combining micro-founded general equilibrium models with policy-relevant economic questions. Prior to studying at ANU, I worked as a Junior Advisor at the Executive Office of the President of Indonesia, where I contributed to policy analysis in issues related to agriculture and labor markets.
